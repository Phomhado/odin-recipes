Burguer

This is a delicious burguer! Burguers are the favorite food of many people
The combination of grilled cheese, the special sauces, tomatoes and onions, and of course, bacon, are a explosion in your mouth


To make a simple burguer, you should have:

  • Buns
  • Beef or other types of meat
  • Cheese (a lot of cheese)
  • Bacon
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce

Here are the step-by-step to make the burguer: (Remember, this is personal, you can try to mount your own burguers!)

  1. Take the beef and put it in your BBQ or in a fry pan with 1 teaspoon of oil
  2. When the beef is medium-rare, put some cheese in top of it.
  3. Then, take a small lid, put some water close to the beef and rapidly close the burguer with the lid
  4. repeat the steps on how many beefs you want (I prefer 2 or 3)
  5. Put the bacon to fry and remove the burguers and cheese to another plate
  6. When the bacon is done, remove it and put the buns "face down" for a little
  7. Cut the onions and tomatoes and put it on top of the meat
  8. Finish with the sauces you want, like ketchup, mayo...

That is how you make a smash burguer! Enjoy
